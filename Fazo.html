Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we may ask for personal information like your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use our services, such as the books you download and the pages you visit.
  • Device Information: We may collect device-specific information such as your hardware model and operating system version.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Send you important notices, such as changes to our terms and policies
  • Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Personalize your experience

Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of Fazo except in the following cases:

  • With your consent: We will share personal information with third parties when we have your consent to do so.
  • For external processing: We provide personal information to our affiliates or other trusted businesses to process it for us.
  • For legal reasons: We will share personal information if we believe disclosure is necessary to comply with applicable laws.

Security

We work hard to protect Fazo and our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information we hold. In particular:

  • We encrypt many of our services using SSL
  • We review our information collection, storage, and processing practices to prevent unauthorized access
  • We restrict access to personal information to Fazo employees who need to know that information to process it

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "effective date" at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

About Us

Our Mission

At 45Tech, we believe that knowledge should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to provide high-quality educational resources to students, professionals, and lifelong learners around the world.

Founded in 2023, Fazo has grown from a small collection of IT notes to a comprehensive platform offering books, study materials, and resources across multiple disciplines.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive Library: Thousands of books and study materials across IT, programming, cybersecurity, and more
  • Affordable Access: Low-cost and free resources to make education accessible
  • Quality Content: All materials are carefully curated and reviewed by experts
  • Mobile Friendly: Access your resources anytime, anywhere

Our Team

45Tech is built by a passionate team of educators, technologists, and content creators dedicated to making learning more accessible.
